# Pittsburgh Hospital Capability Reference

- UPMC Presbyterian: adult Level 1 trauma, burn capability, neurosurgery, orthopedic surgery.
- Allegheny General Hospital: adult Level 1 trauma, neurosurgery, cardiac capability.
- UPMC Mercy: adult trauma and orthopedic surgery.
- UPMC Children's Hospital: pediatric trauma and pediatric surgery.
- Jefferson Hospital: Level 2 trauma and general surgery.
- St. Clair Hospital: community emergency care with limited orthopedic coverage.
