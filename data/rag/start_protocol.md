# START Triage Protocol

- Patients able to walk are generally classified GREEN unless obvious life-threatening findings are reported.
- Adults with respirations greater than 30 per minute should be classified RED.
- Adults with absent radial pulse, no perfusion, or obvious shock findings should be classified RED.
- Adults with inability to follow commands or significantly altered mental status should be classified RED.
- Adults who are not walking but have adequate respirations, perfusion, and mental status should be classified YELLOW.
- Patients who are not breathing after airway repositioning should be classified BLACK.
